About Al-Obaiwani

We are pleased to present to you a brief overview of Al-Obaiwani Company. A contracting company engaged in contracting, road works, excavation, and filling, supplying all types of soil, cleaning waste, and supplying ornamental stones. Since 2007, we have implemented several projects in NEOM, King Abdullah University, King Abdullah Economic City, the housing project, the water project, and the hospital. We are currently implementing many projects in the same field within the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. Our staff numbers 500 qualified, trained, and professional regular staff.
