About Al-OBAYWANI EST

We are pleased to present to you a brief overview of Al-Abiwani EST. A contracting company engaged in contracting, road works, excavation, and filling, supplying all types of soil, cleaning waste, and supplying ornamental stones. Since 2007, we have implemented several projects in NEOM, King Abdullah University, King Abdullah Economic City, the housing project, the water project, and the hospital. We are currently implementing many projects in the same field within the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. Our staff numbers 500 qualified, trained, and professional regular staff.

Our Vision

To become the leading company in the construction sector in Saudi Arabia and the Middle East and to become the first option for the parties, interests, companies, institutions, and individuals. Maximizing the standards of quality and safety as well as increasing our market share in all sectors of the company through strategic alliances with major national and regional companies and remaining the place of confidence and pride for all our clients.

Our Mission

With a growing competitive market, we are committed to providing our clients with the highest quality and service at the best price in all our sectors through our highly qualified and experienced team.
