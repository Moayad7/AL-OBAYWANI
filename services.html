Our Services

At Al-Obaiwani, we offer a wide range of construction services tailored to meet your needs. Our experienced team is dedicated to delivering high-quality results on every project.

Contracting

Comprehensive contracting services that manage and execute construction projects efficiently, ensuring quality, timely completion, and adherence to budget for all your construction needs.

Construction of Residential

Expert residential construction services that transform your vision into reality, focusing on quality craftsmanship and personalized designs for your dream home or renovation project.

Excavation and Backfill

Professional excavation and backfill services that prepare your site safely and efficiently, ensuring a solid foundation for your construction project with precision and care.

Municipal Waste Management

Comprehensive municipal waste management services that ensure efficient collection, recycling, and disposal of waste, promoting sustainability and environmental responsibility in communities.

Soil Supply

High-quality soil supply services offering topsoil, fill dirt, and specialty soils, ensuring you receive the right materials for successful landscaping and construction projects.

Supply of Ornamental Stones

Premium ornamental stones for landscaping and decorative features, available in various colors and textures, enhancing the beauty and appeal of your outdoor spaces.

Water-Related Services

Reliable water-related services that include supply, treatment, and distribution, ensuring safe and clean water for residential, commercial, and industrial needs while adhering to regulations.

Repair and Maintenance of Infrastructure

Expert repair and maintenance services for infrastructure, ensuring longevity and safety through regular inspections, timely repairs, and upgrades to meet evolving community needs.
