Our Services

Al-0baiwani is a premier provider of comprehensive road construction services, delivering end-to-end solutions for highways, urban arterials, rural roads, and industrial transport networks. Our specialized capabilities include: Earthworks & Subgrade Preparation - Precision grading and compaction to engineering specifications Base Course Construction - Installation of stabilized and unbound granular layers Asphalt Paving - Hot-mix and warm-mix applications using advanced paving technologies Concrete Pavement - Rigid pavement construction with jointing and finishing systems Drainage Infrastructure - Culvert installation, stormwater management, and erosion control Pavement Marking & Signage - Road striping, thermoplastic applications, and traffic signage Pavement Rehabilitation - Milling, overlay, and full-depth reclamation services We deliver durable, compliant with all standards. Our certified teams combine engineering precision with operational excellence to complete projects safely, on schedule, and within budget.
