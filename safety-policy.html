HEALTH AND SAFETY POLICY

At Awad Hammoud Al Obaywani Contracting Company (OCC), our commitment extends beyond preventing injuries and illnesses — we are equally dedicated to protecting property, equipment, and the environment.

The foundation of our health and safety policy is the proactive identification and management of risks. We are committed to minimizing risk wherever possible by systematically identifying hazards, assessing potential impacts, and implementing appropriate controls at every stage of our operations.

Maintaining high standards of health and safety enables us to develop our workforce and enhance overall business performance. To support this, we actively promote and participate in initiatives aimed at the continuous improvement of our health and safety management systems, ensuring they are integrated into all aspects of our work.

We fully recognize and adhere to our legal obligations regarding health and safety. However, we view compliance as a baseline—not a limit. We are committed to exceeding these requirements wherever practical. In support of this commitment, we have implemented a certified Occupational Health and Safety Management System in accordance with ISO 45001:2018.

Our ultimate objective is to prevent all injuries, incidents, and environmental harm. We hold our supervisors accountable for the safety and wellbeing of all team members and emphasize that workplace safety is not only a management responsibility but a shared responsibility across the organization. Just as everyone contributes to our operational success, each individual is also responsible for upholding safety standards in every task they undertake.
