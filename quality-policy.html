QUALITY CONTROL POLICY

At Al Obaiwani General Contracting Company (OCC), we are committed to delivering products and services that consistently meet or exceed customer expectations and comply with all applicable standards and regulatory requirements. Our quality control policy is founded on the principles of continuous improvement, employee involvement, and customer satisfaction.

“To Ensure the Highest Level of Quality, OCC:”

  1. Implement and maintain a robust Quality Management System (QMS) aligned with \ [relevant standard, e.g., ISO 9001].
  2. Conduct regular inspections, audits, and testing throughout all stages of production and service delivery
  3. Promote a culture of quality awareness and responsibility across all levels of the organization.
  4. Provide ongoing training and resources to our staff to support excellence in quality control.
  5. Actively seek and incorporate customer feedback to improve our processes and outcomes.
