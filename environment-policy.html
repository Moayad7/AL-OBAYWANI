ENVIRONMENTAL POLICY

At Al Obaiwani Company (OCC), we recognize our responsibility to protect the environment, and we are committed to operating our business in an environmentally sustainable manner. We strive to minimize our environmental impact and continually improve our environmental performance as an integral part of our business strategy and operations.

“OCC Commitments”

  1. Compliance: OCC company will comply with all relevant environmental legislation, regulations, and other requirements.
  2. Pollution Prevention: OCC company aim to prevent pollution by reducing emissions, discharges, and waste through responsible management practices.
  3. Resource Efficiency: OCC company will use energy, water, and other natural resources efficiently and promote sustainable resource use.
  4. Waste Management: OCC company will reduce waste generation, promote reuse and recycling, and ensure the safe disposal of any remaining waste.
  5. Continuous Improvement: OCC company will set environmental objectives and targets, monitor our progress, and review our performance regularly to drive continuous improvement.
  6. Awareness and Training: OCC company will promote environmental awareness among our employees and provide the necessary training to empower them to act responsibly.
  7. Stakeholder Engagement: OCC company will communicate our environmental policy and performance to stakeholders and encourage suppliers and partners to adopt sound environmental practices.
